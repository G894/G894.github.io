利用数据包络分析衡量广西北部湾国际港务集团的港口效率

查看完整 论文

Abstract

With the prosperous development of containerization and the world logistics, the competition between container ports has been undated. The number of throughput and container ship DWT are growing every year. However, the efficiency problem has become one of the elements affect the container port performance. The measurement of a successful container port/port company is more complicated. The throughput data is no longer the only point to measure a container port anymore. Under the efficiency concept, increase the performance by gaining more value from lesser input, has become the key in the container port competition.
DEA also known as Data Envelopment Analysis, it was developed by Charnes (Charnes, Cooper and E. Rhode, 1978). Since last century DEA has been used in measuring organizational efficiency. With contribution of data envelopment analysis in the economic area, DEA has been used by different organization in their area.in recent years, it has become a famous analysis methods.
In this project a basic model in data envelopment analysis will be used to measure a set of samples. Several container ports/port company have been selected as the samples (DMUs), including Shanghai, Ningbo-Zhoushan, Guangzhou, Dalian, Tianjin, Qingdao, Lianyungang and a port company called Guangxi Beibu international Port Group .Ltd (GBIPG .Ltd. The inputs are total berth length, total berths, and total berths with over 10,000DWT docking ability. Total throughput (Millions of TEUs) and total throughput (Millions of Tons) as the outputs. By the end of the case, all the results will be analysed and compared.
