降低离岸风力发电机的维护与运行成本

查看完整 论文

Abstract

The project presents an Excel based linear program model for operation and maintenance cost optimisation in offshore wind farm. This tool required Palisade Evolver to operate. The model is designed to optimise corrective maintenance tasks. In the model, has been designed to schedule a corrective maintenance task within 30 days after a failure occurred. there are few factor in the real world maintenance scheduling have been considered including: model of turbine, capacity factor, downtime cost rate, cost of vessel, cost of employee, weather. A literature review about current offshore wind development, proactive and reactive maintenance strategies, reliability of turbine in sea environment, has been undertaken. The research on the reducing maintenance task cost for offshore wind turbine had to separate in to two main areas proactive and corrective maintenance. During the research, the model has shown the capacity factor is a critical element during the optimisation, it will affect the downtime cost whatever the owner going to execute a maintenance, and the capacity factor is a seasonal floating factor, preventive maintenance should be scheduled more relate to the capacity factor pattern. For the corrective maintenance, the model also shown the booking cost is another critical issue. Sometime in the model the failure with requirement of expensive vessel, operator has to wait until vessel can be booked in a discount price to execute a maintenance task.
